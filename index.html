"My work is a dialogue between the rigid structures of the city and the organic fluidity of memory. I paint not what is seen, but what is felt in the silence between noises."

Working from his studio in the Cape Town Precinct, Austin Mandlokuwa uses locally sourced materials to investigate the changing landscape of post-apartheid South Africa.

Selected Works

Biography

Born in Gweru and trained at the Michaelis School of Fine Art, Austin Mandlokuwa has established himself as a leading voice in contemporary South African art. His work transcends mere representation, diving deep into the psycho-geography of Johannesburg.
